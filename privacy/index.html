Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 15, 2026

At Blakcide, we believe privacy is not a feature; it is a fundamental human right. This policy outlines how we protect your digital sanctuary.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the minimum amount of data required to operate the service:

  • Account Information: When you log in via Google or Apple, we receive your email address and basic profile name to create your account.
  • Sanctuary Data: The chats, notes, and folders you create are stored in our secure database to ensure they are available across your devices.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the Blakcide service to you.

  • We do not sell your personal data to advertisers or third parties.
  • We do not use your private conversation history to train public AI models.

3. Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption (SSL/TLS) for data in transit and secure database storage provided by Supabase. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

4. Your Rights

You have complete control over your sanctuary. You may request the deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us or using the delete function within the app.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@blakcide.com.
